Anya kom Trikru is from the Wood’s clan. An orphan but also a warfare prodigy since she was 13. The best as well as the youngest general in all of the coalition’s lands, but an even prouder warrior in both battle and life. She was notorious for her bad temper, often causing more fights than she admits, both sarcastic and irritating but still the most respected, not to mention skilled general an army could hope for.
