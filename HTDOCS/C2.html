Luna kom Floukru was the second person Kali bonded with since arriving in the Coalition lands. She was her best friend and later her closest confidant. They spent nearly a year together in seclusion within Floukru territory but neither initiated to make their relationship romantic but that was not due to a lack of interest. Kali was one of the few people who Luna told about her past and the darkness she carries since she fled the conclave. It was heartbreaking for Luna when Kali finally left, worst not knowing the reason at all.
